Online advertising or online marketing is a technique of delivering promotional messages to various consumers. Hence it is more or less like forcing the consumer and annoying them with the continue reminder of their product. In spite of lot of interruptions and diversions that happens in people life are increasing, these advertisement becomes an addon. Users argue that they do not want such annoying advertisements on websites because it hampers their area of viewing actual content seamlessly and without obstructions. Hence our adblocker system manages these problems. by using ad blocking methodology in a hardware (Access Point) so that it works as universal ad blocker which consume resources and there is no need for managing different software’s on different devices.
